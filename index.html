Founder Story

Why BridgeCare Exists

Hi, I'm Ridhima Kapoor, Founder of BridgeCare.

BridgeCare began with a simple observation: while many seniors experience loneliness, working parents increasingly struggle to find trusted childcare.

By bringing generations together, we can create communities where children thrive, seniors find renewed purpose and families gain the support they need.
